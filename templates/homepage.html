On The Job

What's it really like?

Inside Australia's modern manufacturing workplaces, you'll see collaboration in action, creative problem-solving and impressive technology like robotics, 3D printing and advanced computing.

From designing life-saving medical devices to building clean energy solutions, you'll be part of something tangible and vital. It's where hands-on work meets high-tech innovation.
