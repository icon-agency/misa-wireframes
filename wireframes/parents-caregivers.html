Future-Focused Careers? Fact

As you support your teen to consider their future, you'll see that manufacturing offers diverse, secure and rewarding careers. Today's industry is driven by innovation and technology, which is actively changing the way we live in our communities. From improving health care with the manufacturing of personalised medicines to the adoption of robotics to increase safe and efficient ways of working, manufacturing is revolutionising and transforming our world.

Stability & Job Security

Manufacturing not only drives economic growth but also fosters innovation across a range of industries. As technology advances and products become increasingly complex, the need for highly trained professionals continues to rise, creating stable career pathways, competitive salaries and opportunities.

A Variety Of Education Paths

Apprenticeships are just one way to enter into a career in manufacturing. Through an apprenticeship, your teen can earn their qualifications, gain experience and earn a wage. Higher education offers an alternative path to a number of careers, leading to reliable and in-demand jobs.

Diverse & Engaging Roles

From design to engineering and logistics, there's a role for most interests. Problem-solving, innovation, and creativity are attributes in demand for those seeking a career in manufacturing.

Opportunities Across Key Sectors

Australia's manufacturing industry is undergoing significant transformation within sectors such as general manufacturing and engineering, process manufacturing, aerospace and laboratory operations. Strong job opportunities are emerging as a result of advanced technology adoption — like automation and digitalisation — as well as a national drive for greater sustainability and sovereign capability.
